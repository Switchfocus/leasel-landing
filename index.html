02

The mechanics of the Luxembourg rental market are not in the tenant's favour.

15–20applications

A listing receives 15–20 applications within 48 hours.

A well-priced apartment in Luxembourg City is taken in one to three weeks; in Kirchberg or Limpertsberg, often within days. Speed matters more than fit.

47%

Nearly half the workforce is cross-border or expat.

225,000 cross-border workers and a foreign resident population of 47% means most renters arrive without a local network, a French CDI, or a familiar dossier format.

~2%

Vacancy below two percent. Below 1.5% in prime areas.

One of the tightest residential markets in Europe. Landlords screen on signal quality, not patience. The slowest applicant loses by default.

Build

Verify

Share

Send a single link per application. Each share is logged, time-limited, and revocable. The agency receives a verified dossier; you keep the audit trail.
